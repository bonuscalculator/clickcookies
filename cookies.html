Cookies & Storage Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

This Cookies Policy explains how ClickCookies uses cookies, tracking pixels, and client-side web storage architecture solutions when you interact with our application interface at clickcookies.com.

1. Cookies vs. Local Storage

While traditional websites use server-transmitted HTTP cookies for traffic identification data patterns, ClickCookies uses browser-native Web Local Storage variables for primary gameplay processing configurations. Unlike transient cookies that expire or communicate data structures continuously to external remote systems, Local Storage keeps your core session data directly inside your browser cache. This enables instant read-write execution times and complete progress safety without remote performance lag.

2. How We Use Caching and Storage Mechanics

Our client-side deployment handles your interactive parameters exclusively through these technical classifications:

  • Essential Progress Saving: Manages core variables including total cookies baked, building inventory, current cookies-per-second, and unlocked achievements. Without these client-side strings, your gaming milestones would reset on every tab refresh.
  • UI Customization Preference: Maintains persistent display flags, mobile navigation toggle settings, interactive visual elements state conditions, and audio-visual configuration markers.
  • Third-Party Metrics & Promotion Trackers: Our integrated ad networks and traffic diagnostics tools leverage minimal diagnostic tracking blocks to ensure valid ad visibility reporting frameworks and structural stability indicators.

3. Third-Party Advertising Vendors

We may partner with advertising networks to sustain our free utility framework. These providers utilize automated identification files to show personalized promotional campaigns based on your historical browsing journeys across disparate applications. You maintain autonomous tracking configuration authority and can toggle tracking filters by utilizing native industry programmatic opt-out panels.

4. Managing and Clearing Your Cache Data

If you prefer to disable storage frameworks, you can adjust your browser layout parameters to block tracking records or drop tracking structures automatically. However, selecting this storage configuration route will permanently disconnect our script's baseline logic, rendering the idle progression mechanics and automated asset loops unfunctional.

To explicitly clear out your game configurations, navigate to your platform configuration settings and execute a "Clear Website Data" sweep for clickcookies.com, or leverage our internal "Reset Progress" functionality within the interface.
